Welcome to FeedbackFlow

Your go-to app for providing meaningful and structured feedback on student essays.

About This App

FeedbackFlow is designed to streamline the process of reviewing and providing feedback on student writing. Key features include:

  • Automated, AI-powered feedback on student essays
  • Organized assignment management for teachers
  • Secure storage of essays and feedback for future reference
  • Customizable focus areas for targeted feedback

Data Policy

FeedbackFlow collects and stores the following data:

  • Submitted essays and associated feedback
  • Assignment details created by teachers
  • Basic user information such as email

This data is solely used to enable the functionality of the app. It is securely stored and not shared with any third parties or used beyond the app's intended purpose.

How It Works

Using FeedbackFlow is simple:

  1. Create or select an assignment from the dashboard.
  2. Submit a student's essay to generate AI-powered feedback.
  3. Review and share the feedback with your students.
  4. Access all past essays and feedback in the assignment history.
